About EduScore

EduScore is an online platform dedicated to enhancing the educational environment of De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde (DLS-CSB) by making educator evaluations accessible to the entire community. Our platform aims to bridge the gap in transparency, accountability, and accessibility of evaluation data, promoting a culture of continuous improvement and accountability.
